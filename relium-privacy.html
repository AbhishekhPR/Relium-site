Privacy & Data Handling

Your data never
leaves your warehouse.

Relium runs entirely within your database. We don't move data off-premise, store sensitive values, or build profiles on you.

On-Premise by Default

All analysis happens inside your database. No data transfer, no cloud uploads. We connect via your database connection string and execute SQL queries that you can audit.

Metadata Only

Relium reads table structures, row counts, column names, and freshness timestamps. We never export actual data rows, customer records, or PII.

No Profiling

We don't build behavioral profiles, track your team members, or analyze your business logic. We measure quality anomalies — nothing more.

Your Incident History

Incident reports and anomaly logs stay in your control. Optional Slack integration requires a webhook URL you provide and control.
