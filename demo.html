Root cause analysis

Finding

Null-excluding filter moved from join condition into WHERE clause, changing LEFT JOIN behavior.

Evidence

Customer rows without matching orders are removed before downstream revenue aggregation.

Blast radius

Revenue dashboard, retention metrics, executive revenue readout, and weekly board report may be wrong.

Suggested fix

Move the filter back into the JOIN condition or explicitly preserve unmatched customer rows.
